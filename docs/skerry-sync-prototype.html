timerExpires in 4:32 · single use

How pairing works

1This device generates a one-time transferKey and shows it as QR + code. The key itself never reaches the server.
2The new device scans the QR (or you type the code) and derives the same transferKey locally.
3dataKey is encrypted with transferKey and relayed through sync.mylab.dev — the server sees only ciphertext with a 5-minute TTL.
4The new device decrypts dataKey, registers itself, and pulls the full vault. Hardware-backed keys are not transferred — re-enroll them on the new device.
5Alternative: simply log in with your master password on the new device — SRP login, then the wrapped dataKey is fetched and unwrapped locally.
